# Topic: Hobbies — Question Set (20)

Numbered for random selection. 1–10 easy / 11–17 medium / 18–20 hard.

1. What do you usually do in your free time?
2. What hobby are you into these days?
3. How often do you play video games?
4. What was the last book, show, or game you really enjoyed?
5. Do you prefer spending your days off at home or going out? Why?
6. Is there a hobby you do every week without fail?
7. What did you like doing when you were a kid?
8. Do you enjoy your hobbies alone or with other people?
9. How much time do you spend on your hobby in a typical week?
10. What hobby would you recommend to a busy person?
11. How did you first get into your main hobby?
12. Has any hobby of yours ever helped you at work?
13. What's something about your hobby that most people misunderstand?
14. Have you ever quit a hobby? What made you stop?
15. If you had a completely free weekend and no budget limit, how would you spend it?
16. What's a hobby you'd love to try but haven't yet? What's stopping you?
17. Do you think hobbies should stay fun, or is it good to take them seriously?
18. Some people say turning a hobby into a side job ruins the fun. What do you think?
19. How have your hobbies changed as you've gotten older, and what does that say about you?
20. If you could master one skill instantly — related to any hobby — what would you choose, and what would you do with it?
